# Artist panel — quick reference

Operational notes for artists using the Insomnia Tattoo dashboard. For technical setup (accounts, roles), contact the studio admin.

## Bookings

- Review new requests in **Artist → Bookings**. Update status when you contact the client, confirm the session, or close the case.
- Clients may upload reference images; keep files inside the platform rather than sharing personal channels for booking data unless the client prefers otherwise.

## Availability

- Maintain accurate slots under **Artist → Availability** so the public booking calendar stays trustworthy.

## Gallery & profile

- **Gallery** and **Profile** feed the public artist pages. Keep bios and portfolio images up to date and consistent with what you offer.

## Loyalty & reviews

- Loyalty and reviews (where enabled) tie to completed work and client accounts. Confirm the correct booking before applying adjustments.

## Support

- For access issues, wrong role, or bugs, email the studio technical contact (see internal runbooks). Do not share JWT cookies or passwords.
